Key Information

About the PMI: The ISM Manufacturing PMI® is a composite index based on five indicators: New Orders (30%), Production (25%), Employment (20%), Supplier Deliveries (15%), and Inventories (10%).

Reading above 50: Indicates expansion in the manufacturing sector
Reading below 50: Indicates contraction in the manufacturing sector

Economic Indicator: A Manufacturing PMI® above 42.3% over time generally indicates expansion of the overall economy (GDP).
